Jixin Li is a research scientist, now enrolled in Northeastern University in Boston. He received master degree in Statistics at Columbia University in 2018, B.A. degree with a major in general psychology and minor in applied statistics at Univeristy of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 2014. After graduation from college, he spent some time in China working as a project manager in nonprofits in underprivileged children education. His life and work experiences inspired him to pursue meaningful application of big data techniques in the analysis and research of human behavior. His research instrerests include understanding personality development and appying information techonologies in serving the mental health of underserved people at a low cost.
